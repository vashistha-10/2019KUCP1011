In the first semester of the IIIT Kota reign, I have worked on the project or rather an Algorithm named as ANT LION ALGORITHM. The Ant Lion Optimizer, known as ALO or Antlion Optimizer, is a recent meta-heuristic that mathematically models the interaction of ants and antlions in nature. An optimization algorithm has been developed to solve optimization problems considering random walk of ants, building traps, entrapment of ants in traps, catching preys, and re-building traps are implemented.
